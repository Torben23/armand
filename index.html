The Longevity Formula for Men Over 50: How to be 50 & Counting and Live a Happy, Healthy & Long Life.

Remember when you were in your mid-20s and you thought, “50? That’s so far away! I have tons of time! I don't have to worry about that yet.” Then BOOM, you wake up one day and you’re in your 50s. You feel the weight of time. Now, every passing second is taking you closer to the end of this incredible experience of life. The Longevity Formula for Men Over 50 is a book about health, happiness, and longevity. This book is based on a holistic approach for men over 50 and will guide you through a new perspective on your upcoming years. This is about aging versus getting old. This is about energy, joy, and the lightness of being you. This is about reclaiming your power and forging the best moments of your life with your partner, immediate family, and friends. These are my experiences and strategies of living more than 55 years pill-free and pain-free. Are you ready to live healthily, happily, and longer?

Health Coaching

Discover deeper meaning in your life and break-free from bad food habits that are no longer serving you. Discover new flavors, new dishes and get fit, lighter, energized, healthy and positive.

Body Coaching

Keep your body healthy and in constant motion without having to strain it. I personally am not interested in lifting weights or taking any supplement to enhance and trigger muscle growth. I prefer to combine different forms of exercise to get the same result. It will be easier to live through the ups and downs of life with a body shape and muscle mass you are comfortable with rather than building up an appearance that may not be sustainable.

Lifestyle Coaching

Overcome your limiting beliefs and scarcity mindset to find your inner voice and create a heart-centered life. Once you reach 50, it is beneficial to take time to reflect on your life and to keep the good stuff, unload and set aside the poor habits and rebuild yourself for a new launch of your better self.
